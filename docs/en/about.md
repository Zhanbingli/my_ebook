# About This Knowledge Base

## 🩺 About Dr. Li Zhanbing

Welcome to my personal knowledge repository - a dynamic intersection where medical practice meets artificial intelligence, and traditional learning evolves into collaborative knowledge creation.

### Professional Background

As a medical professional deeply immersed in both clinical practice and academic research, I've witnessed firsthand the transformative power of technology in healthcare. My journey spans:

- **Clinical Practice**: Years of hands-on medical experience, treating patients and navigating complex clinical scenarios
- **Academic Research**: Engagement with cutting-edge medical research and evidence-based practice
- **Educational Innovation**: Exploring new paradigms in medical education and knowledge transfer
- **AI Integration**: Investigating how artificial intelligence can enhance rather than replace human medical judgment

### Vision & Mission

**Vision**: To create a living knowledge ecosystem where medical expertise, AI capabilities, and educational innovation converge to serve human health and advance medical practice.

**Mission**: 
- Share insights from the intersection of medicine and AI
- Document the journey from knowledge consumer to knowledge producer
- Foster critical thinking and evidence-based reasoning
- Build bridges between traditional medical practice and emerging technologies
